Global Her Project

2019

This 3 1/2 minute musical film informs viewers about the Global Gag Rule (aka Mexico City Policy) and calls for real, legislative change that can end the harmful policy. Congress reintroduced the Global HER Act in June of 2021, a bill that could end this policy, and we can help pass it by calling our reps today in support of the Global HER Act. This project was made in collaboration with organizations including PAI, CHANGE, and The National Women’s Law Center. Please visit www.globalherproject.com for more.
